  Find the current positions of the green diamond and red circle.

  If the action is a directional move (UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT):
    Calculate the agent's potential new position.
    If the new position is not a wall:
      Move the agent to the new position.
      Move the green diamond by the same direction and distance as the agent.

  If the action is WAIT:
    // Green Diamond seeks the cell to the right of the agent
    Define the diamond's target position as (agent_row, agent_col + 1).
    If the diamond is not at its target, move it one step towards the target (prioritizing horizontal movement).

    // Red Circle follows a fixed patrol route
    Based on the circle's current position, determine its next step on its rectangular path.
    If the circle's destination contains a yellow star, the star is removed.
    Move the circle to its new position.

  Return the updated grid and the agent's new position.
