Provider: fal / Model: google/gemini-2.5-pro
Episode: full_complex
Actions: ['RIGHT', 'DOWN', 'DOWN', 'DOWN', 'LEFT', 'UP', 'UP']

Procedure to apply an action:
  Count all green triangles currently on the grid. Let this count be N.

  If N is zero:
    The agent does not move and the grid does not change.
  Else (if N is greater than zero):
    The agent's move distance is equal to N.
    The direction of movement is determined by the input action.
    Calculate the agent's new position by moving N steps in the chosen direction.

    Update the grid state:
      Move the agent from its old position to the new one.
      Remove ALL green triangles from the grid, turning their cells empty.
